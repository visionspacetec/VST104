* MAX6495 MACROMODEL
* ------------------------------
* Revision 0, 4/2008
* ------------------------------
* MAX6495 is a small, low-current, overvoltage-protection circuit for high-voltage transient systems such as those found in automotive and industrial applications. These devices monitor the input voltage and control an external n-channel MOSFET switch to either isolate the load or to limit the voltage to the loadat the output during an input overvoltage condition. It operates over a wide supply voltage range from +5.5V to +72V.
